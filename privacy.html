None. Baby Names Finder works entirely on your device using a bundled names database. No data is collected, transmitted, or shared with anyone.

This app uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Text-to-speech is handled by your device's built-in engine.

Saved or favourite names are stored locally on your device only and are never sent externally.
